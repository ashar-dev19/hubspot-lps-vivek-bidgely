USE CASES

What UtilityAI™ makes possible in electrification.

Each use case uses the same appliance-level intelligence layer, so your DSM, grid, and marketing teams operate from the same source of intelligence.

Heat Pump Targeting and Recruitment

Find who will benefit most and is ready to move — before you spend a dollar on outreach.

Utilities running broad heat pump campaigns reach too many unqualified households and miss the ones most likely to convert. Bidgely identifies homes currently running gas or electric resistance heating, scores them by propensity, highest inefficiency, longest running hours, and income eligibility — and surfaces them for targeted outreach at the point of highest intent.
  • 6X measured ROI in initial heat pump detection deployment
  • Heat pump detection validated with ground truth from four North American utilities
Explore Heat Pump Targeting

Electric Space Heater & Water Heater Targeting

Replace the highest-cost appliances first — with precision.

Gas-operated or aging space heaters and water heaters represent some of the richest targets for electrification. Bidgely detects them from AMI data without a survey, segments by upgrade readiness, and feeds targeted lists directly into your DSM workflow.
  • Appliance-level detection of electric resistance and legacy gas water heaters
  • Segmentation by upgrade readiness, income eligibility, and program fit
  • Direct hand-off to DSM and marketing workflows
Explore Space & Water Heater Targeting

EV L2 Charger Rebates & TOU Rates

Get ahead of home charging load before it concentrates on your grid.

EV adoption without load management creates peak loading problems utilities discover too late. Bidgely identifies households with EVs already present, flags L1/L2 charger type, and identifies households likely to acquire an EV within 12–18 months — so you can enroll or educate them into EV-TOU rates or managed charging before the load appears.
  • 90%+ EV detection accuracy from AMI data alone
  • 10,000+ EVs identified with AMI data only across multiple utilities
  • 3X EV program enrollment and load shift realized
  • 70%+ peak charging load shifted across reference utilities
Explore EV Programs
