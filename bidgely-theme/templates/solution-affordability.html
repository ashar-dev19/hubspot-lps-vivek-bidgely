01  LMI IDENTIFICATION

LMI Household Identification & Scoring

Find who qualifies — without asking them to raise their hand.

Most LMI programs rely on customers to self-identify or cross-reference LIHEAP rolls. Bidgely builds an income-proxy score from AMI data, census overlays, and appliance usage patterns — identifying eligible households passively and continuously.
  • 90%+ precision on LMI identification without self-disclosure
  • Refreshed scores as household energy behavior changes
  • Integrates directly with CRM and program enrollment workflows

Bill Assistance & LIHEAP Enrollment

Drive enrollment before bills become crisis.

High-burden households are often enrolled in bill assistance programs only after they've already fallen behind. Bidgely identifies rising energy burden early — flagging households for proactive outreach before arrears accumulate.
  • Early-warning flags for rising energy cost burden
  • Segmented by program eligibility, income tier, and usage pattern

03  WEATHERIZATION

Weatherization & Efficiency Program Targeting

Target upgrades where they'll have the biggest impact on energy burden.

Weatherization dollars go furthest when they're concentrated on homes with the highest heating and cooling loads. Bidgely's appliance-level profiles identify inefficient HVAC, aging water heaters, and envelope losses — prioritizing upgrade candidates by both energy burden and program ROI.
  • Appliance-level identification of high-load, low-efficiency homes
