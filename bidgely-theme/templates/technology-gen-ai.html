WHAT YOU CAN BUILD

Grounded answers for every team

Every GenAI interface runs on the same pre-trained models and the same deployment your CIO already approved.

For Customer Experience and Call Center

  • A CSR asks why a customer's bill spiked and gets an appliance-level breakdown instantly, while still on the call
  • Customers ask why their bills have gone up and get common-sense explanations grounded in their actual usage
  • A CSR asks which rate plan would save a specific customer money and gets a recommendation based on real-world use
  • A manager asks about program enrollment trends across customer segments in real time

For Grid Planning and Operations

  • A planner asks which feeders are most exposed to EV load growth over the next three years
  • An engineer asks about transformer loading history without writing a line of code
  • An analyst asks for a summary of DER penetration by substation across the entire service territory
  • The operations team asks about outage patterns correlated with load and equipment age

For Regulatory and Rates

  • A rate analyst asks for appliance-level elasticity data to inform rate design without a manual data pull
  • An analyst asks about energy burden by premises to identify income-qualified customers for affordability programs
  • A regulatory team builds an interactive cost-benefit analysis for a filing by querying program and load data
  • An analyst asks for Integrated Resource Planning (IRP) scenario inputs without waiting on a data team
