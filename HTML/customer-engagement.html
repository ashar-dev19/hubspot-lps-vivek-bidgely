Proven Results

Deployed at scale. Measured at the meter.

Avista

Identified 160,000+ eligible IQ households to transform enrollment outreach from broad to hyper-targeted. 7X improvement in per-customer savings using AWB-targeted Home Energy Audits vs. legacy approach (1 MWh vs. 0.1 MWh per customer).

OG&E

Enterprise CX across ~650k residential and 132k SMB customers. 5.5M+ emails sent in Oklahoma at 58% open rate; 262k+ in Arkansas at 58% open rate. Identified ~900 VPP customers increasing peak usage during critical events, then reduced that group by 72% through targeted coaching. ~10,000 top-performing SmartHours customers driving >28 MW of peak demand reduction. 1,000+ EE campaign leads generated in two weeks (up from ~50/day baseline). 97 MW SmartHours savings at 114% of target.
